We Provide free lavalink hosting for everyone.

Lavalink is a standalone audio sending node based on Lavaplayer and JDA-Audio.
Allows for sending audio without it ever reaching any of your shards. It is a great alternative to Youtube API.







Server's
Currently our server is hosted at Hetzner in Finland.
